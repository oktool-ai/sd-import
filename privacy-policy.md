# Privacy Policy — SD Import

**Last Updated: March 28, 2026**

## Overview

SD Import ("the App") is a camera media import tool developed by OKTool. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

**In short: We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal data.**

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- We do not collect location data
- We do not collect device identifiers or advertising IDs
- We do not collect usage analytics or behavioral data
- We do not access your photos or videos beyond the import process
- We do not upload any files to external servers

## How the App Works

SD Import operates entirely on your device. The App:

1. **Reads files from your SD card** — Only when you explicitly select a directory via the system file picker. Files are read locally and never transmitted anywhere.
2. **Saves to your Photos library** — Imported files are written directly to the iOS system Photos app on your device. We use the "Add Only" photo library permission, meaning the App can only save new files — it cannot read, modify, or delete your existing photos.
3. **Stores preferences locally** — Language preference and Pro purchase status are saved in the App's local storage (UserDefaults) on your device only.

## In-App Purchases

SD Import offers a one-time Pro upgrade via Apple's App Store. All purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. We do not process or store any payment information.

## Third-Party Services

SD Import does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or cloud services. The App functions completely offline.

## Children's Privacy

SD Import does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above.

# 隐私政策 — SD卡导入

**最后更新：2026年3月28日**

## 概述

SD卡导入（以下简称"本应用"）是由 OKTool 开发的相机素材导入工具。本隐私政策说明我们在您使用本应用时如何处理您的信息。

**简而言之：我们不收集、存储或分享您的任何个人数据。**

## 我们不收集的信息

- 我们不收集个人信息（姓名、邮箱、手机号等）
- 我们不收集位置数据
- 我们不收集设备标识符或广告 ID
- 我们不收集使用分析或行为数据
- 我们不会在导入过程之外访问您的照片或视频
- 我们不会将任何文件上传到外部服务器

## 应用如何运作

SD卡导入完全在您的设备上运行。本应用：

1. **读取 SD 卡文件** — 仅在您通过系统文件选择器明确选择目录时读取文件。文件在本地读取，不会传输到任何地方。
2. **保存到系统相册** — 导入的文件直接写入 iOS 系统照片应用。我们使用"仅添加"相册权限，这意味着应用只能保存新文件，无法读取、修改或删除您现有的照片。
3. **本地存储偏好设置** — 语言偏好和 Pro 购买状态仅保存在您设备上的应用本地存储（UserDefaults）中。

## 应用内购买

SD卡导入提供一次性 Pro 升级，通过 Apple App Store 完成。所有购买交易完全由 Apple 处理，我们不处理或存储任何支付信息。

## 第三方服务

SD卡导入不集成任何第三方 SDK、分析工具、广告网络或云服务。本应用完全离线运行。

## 儿童隐私

SD卡导入不会有意收集 13 岁以下儿童的信息。
